Sourei AutoNotify is a WordPress plugin that integrates WhatsApp notifications to your WooCommerce store. It sends automatic messages to your customers when their orders status changes. You can also send custom messages to individual customers, and manage all notifications from your WordPress dashboard.

== Description ==

Sourei AutoNotify is a plugin that simplifies communication with your customers by sending automatic WhatsApp messages to their mobile phones. The plugin integrates with your WooCommerce store, and sends notifications to your customers when their order status changes.

With Sourei AutoNotify, you can customize your messages, and send targeted promotions or important updates to your customers. You can also send messages to individual customers, and manage all your notifications from your WordPress dashboard.

Features:

Automated WhatsApp notifications for your WooCommerce store
Customizable message templates
Send custom messages to individual customers
Manage all notifications from your WordPress dashboard

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install Sourei AutoNotify? =

You can install Sourei AutoNotify just like any other WordPress plugin. Go to your WordPress dashboard, click on Plugins > Add New, and search for "Sourei AutoNotify". Click on "Install Now", and then "Activate".

= Can I customize the messages that are sent to my customers? =

Yes, you can customize your message templates and create your own messages to send to individual customers.

= Can I manage all notifications from my WordPress dashboard? =

Yes, with Sourei AutoNotify, you can manage all your notifications from your WordPress dashboard.
